Fried Chicken Wings

Description

Crispy, golden-brown chicken wings, with a perfectly crunchy exterior and juicy, tender meat within. A symphony of flavors awaits as you savor each bite, whether coated in fiery hot sauce, tangy barbecue glaze, or dusted with savory spices.

What’s not to love about these fried chicken wings? They’re crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, and bursting with irresistible flavor. This fried chicken wing recipe will quickly become a tried and true favorite.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this restaurant-worthy fried chicken wing recipe:

  • Chicken: This recipe makes 12 chicken wings, but it’s easy to double or even triple it for a larger crowd.
  • Seasonings: What makes these fried chicken wings so flavorful? A combination of seasoned salt, coarse salt, black pepper, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  • Flour: Coat the wings in a seasoned all-purpose flour mixture before frying for extra crispy skin.
  • Oil: Use a neutral oil with a high smoke point, such as vegetable oil, to fry the chicken wings.
  • Sauce: Use store-bought Buffalo wing sauce or make your own at home.

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the seasonings, flour, and oil.
  2. Heat the oil over medium heat in a large skillet or wok.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken wings to the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and crispy on the outside.
  4. Once the chicken wings are cooked, drain and transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the Buffalo wing sauce with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  7. Add the seasoned chicken wings to the skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Once the chicken wings are cooked, drain and transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Combine the fried chicken wings with the Buffalo wing sauce in a serving bowl.
  10. Serve hot with a side of crispy bread or a salad.
  11. Enjoy your fried chicken wings!
