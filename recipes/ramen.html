Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

Ramen noodle soup is a popular Japanese dish that's made with noodles, vegetables, and a soy sauce.

This simple recipe will make a delicious and satisfying meal for your family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable broth, soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger root over medium heat. Stir in the sesame oil and cook for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the ramen noodles to the pot and cook for another 5-7 minutes, until they are al done.
  3. Serve the ramen noodle soup with the green onions and enjoy!
