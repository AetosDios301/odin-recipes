Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

A delicious, refreshing chocolate smoothie that's perfect for busy weekdays or weekends. This recipe combines the sweetness of chocolate pudding with the creaminess of peanut butter, making it an easy and satisfying snack option.

Ingredients will vary depending on your preference for chocolate flavor, but this recipe is perfect for a warm, comforting, and satisfying snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 (3.25 ounce) cups Snack Pack® Chocolate Pudding
  • 2 tablespoons Peter Pan® Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 2 large ripe bananas, cut into pieces
  • ¾ cup reduced fat (2%) milk
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Reddi-wip® Chocolate Dairy Whipped Topping

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the chocolate pudding, peanut butter, bananas, milk, and ice cubes.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the smoothie has thickened slightly.
  3. Once the smoothie has t hickened, stir in the Reddi-wip® chocolate dairy whipped topping.
  4. Serve the smoothie immediately, garnished with a sprinkle of chopped red pepper, if desired.
